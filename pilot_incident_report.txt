INCIDENT DESCRIPTION
--------------------
During cruise at FL350 (35,000 feet), the aircraft experienced severe
turbulence over the Rajasthan region at coordinates 26.5N 74.2E.
The autopilot disconnected automatically. Airspeed at time of incident
was 480 knots. The aircraft lost 800 feet of altitude within 12 seconds.

WEATHER CONDITIONS
------------------
Visibility    : 10km
Wind          : 340 degrees at 45 knots at cruise altitude
Cloud cover   : CB clouds at FL320 to FL380
Turbulence    : Severe Clear Air Turbulence (CAT)
OAT           : -52 degrees Celsius at FL350

CREW ACTIONS TAKEN
------------------
1. Captain disengaged autopilot and took manual control
2. Co-pilot issued MAYDAY call on frequency 121.5 MHz
3. Seat belt signs activated immediately
4. Speed reduced to 320 knots
5. Descent initiated to FL280 to exit turbulence zone
6. ATC Delhi notified, diverted to Jaipur Airport (VIJP)
7. Emergency landing completed at 15:48 IST

CAUSE OF INCIDENT
